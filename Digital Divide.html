Root Causes
Why does the divide persist?
01
Infrastructure Gap
600 million people live in areas with zero mobile network coverage. Telecoms won't invest where profit is impossible.
02
The Cost Barrier
In the poorest nations, basic broadband costs over 20% of monthly income. Devices remain luxury items — not tools.
It's not just about hardware.
It's about Literacy.
Owning a device is useless without the ability to evaluate information, navigate an OS, or protect your own data and privacy online.
